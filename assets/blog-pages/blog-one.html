About this website

Hi there, thanks for stopping by.

I wanted to let you into a little secret… I have no idea what I’m doing, but who does? This includes how to write a blog or how to build a website. Actually, that last one's a lie. I built this one. However, there are a couple of points I’d like to make.

Numero Uno, and the main reason for this site is to learn in public. There’s a great YouTube discussion here. I listened to a while back, which encapsulates exactly what I want to achieve whilst learning Web Development. Paul and Mark talk through the benefits and how “Learning in public” is not always that. We often see a polished completed version of “how I already accomplished this (thing)” leaving out the blood, sweat and tears it took to get there. I’m giving you all of it, The good, the bad, and the ugly.

In an article by swyx about why we should share our learning process, he said, “Make the thing you wish you had found when you were learning". You can read the article here.

What do I want to find?

I would love to find someone learning web development that actually talks about the challenging times. Or a senior Web developer when they first started out. I’d like to know the hours it took them to realize the problem was a missing closing tag or a typo. Seeing them look as confused as I do when trying to decipher MDN docs, or the dreaded git errors. I want to know that I’m not alone in my mistakes, frustrations and achievements.

Therefore, I want to share as much of my learning journey as I can. So basically I’m making a fool of myself on the internet to make you feel better about yourself.

Numero dos, I’m a perfectionist and very indecisive about building websites, especially this one. The third build is almost done, but I no longer like it. I want to change everything again. Moreover, I have no design abilities whatsoever. If I made my design decisions, this site would look like a 5-year-old built it.

Given all the above, the site will change at some point, there are things still to be added, like a comment system. If you find a section that looks familiar please don’t sue me. I’m working on it.

Feel free to provide constructive criticism via email my below.

Thanks for visiting, it’s been nice. I hope you pop by again soon.

Toodle-pip!
